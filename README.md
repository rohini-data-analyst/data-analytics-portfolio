# HR-Dashboard
# 📊 HR Analytics Dashboard (Power BI Project)

## 📌 Overview
This project is an interactive HR Analytics Dashboard built using Power BI. It provides insights into employee demographics, salary distribution, work experience, and job satisfaction levels.

The dashboard helps HR teams and management make data-driven decisions.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze total employee distribution
- Understand gender diversity
- Study salary segmentation
- Evaluate employee experience
- Measure job satisfaction levels

---

## 📊 Key Insights

### 👥 Employee Overview
- Total Employees: 450
- Male: 259
- Female: 191

### 📈 Age Group Analysis
- Majority employees are in the *26–35 age group*
- Younger workforce (18–25) is comparatively lower

### 💰 Salary Distribution
- Highest employees fall under *5k–10k salary slab*
- Fewer employees in higher salary brackets (15k+)

### 🧑‍💼 Experience Analysis
- Most employees have *5–10 years experience*
- Very few employees with more than 15 years

### 😊 Job Satisfaction
- Majority employees fall under *Medium satisfaction*
- Lower satisfaction group indicates improvement areas

# Sales-Dashboard
 Sales Performance Dashboard (Power BI)
🚀 Project Overview
This project presents a dynamic Sales Dashboard built using Power BI to analyze and visualize business performance across multiple dimensions such as sales, profit, quantity, region, and product categories.

The dashboard enables stakeholders to quickly understand key metrics, track trends, and make data-driven decisions effectively.

🎯 Key Objectives
Monitor overall Sales, Profit, and Quantity KPIs

Analyze profit contribution by product category

Track month-over-month (MoM) sales trends

Compare regional sales performance

Evaluate target achievement vs actual performance

Identify high and low-performing products using profit margin analysis

📌 Dashboard Highlights
KPI Cards

Total Sales: 2M

Total Profit: 417K

Total Quantity: 347

Category Analysis

Profit distribution across Electronics, Clothing, and Furniture

Trend Analysis

Monthly sales trend to identify growth and decline patterns

Regional Performance

Sales comparison across South, West, North, and East regions

Target vs Achievement

Gauge chart showing performance against sales targets

Profit Margin Insights

Product-level margin comparison for better pricing strategy

🛠️ Tools & Technologies Used
Power BI – Data visualization and dashboard creation

Power Query – Data transformation and cleaning

DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) – Calculated measures and KPIs

Excel / CSV Dataset – Data source

📈 Key Insights
Electronics category generates the highest profit contribution

Sales show a declining trend over months, indicating potential business challenges

South region leads in sales performance

Overall target achievement exceeds expectations (~121%)

Certain products maintain higher profit margins, indicating strong profitability

💡 Business Impact
This dashboard helps businesses to:

Identify growth opportunities and weak areas

Improve sales strategy and regional focus

Optimize product pricing and profitability

Make real-time, data-driven decisions
