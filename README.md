# HR-Dashboard
# 📊 HR Analytics Dashboard (Power BI Project)

## 📌 Overview
This project is an interactive HR Analytics Dashboard built using Power BI. It provides insights into employee demographics, salary distribution, work experience, and job satisfaction levels.

The dashboard helps HR teams and management make data-driven decisions.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze total employee distribution
- Understand gender diversity
- Study salary segmentation
- Evaluate employee experience
- Measure job satisfaction levels

## 📊 Key Insights

### 👥 Employee Overview
- Total Employees: 450
- Male: 259
- Female: 191

### 📈 Age Group Analysis
- Majority employees are in the *26–35 age group*
- Younger workforce (18–25) is comparatively lower

### 💰 Salary Distribution
- Highest employees fall under *5k–10k salary slab*
- Fewer employees in higher salary brackets (15k+)

### 🧑‍💼 Experience Analysis
- Most employees have *5–10 years experience*
- Very few employees with more than 15 years

### 😊 Job Satisfaction
- Majority employees fall under *Medium satisfaction*
- Lower satisfaction group indicates improvement areas
